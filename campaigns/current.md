# Purpose

Why does this campaign exist?

- Concentrate attention on the two highest-leverage levers for long-term freedom right now: replacing the current income ceiling with a higher-paying role, and proving independent building capability by shipping a real product. Personal Brand and the Opportunity Fund compound quietly in the background. Everything else is protected, not advanced.

# Success Criteria

The campaign is considered successful when:

- [ ] System design project (preferably applied AI) built and published, demonstrating system design competence
- [ ] Expense Tracker MVP shipped and usable end-to-end in production
- [ ] At least one Personal Brand artifact published per week, sustained across the campaign
- [ ] ₹32,000/month consistently set aside into the Opportunity Fund for the duration of the campaign
